# VIRIVDA-ANNUAL-REPORT-DASHBOARD-EXCEL-
This project analyzes the Vrinda Store sales dataset to identify key trends in business performance. After cleaning and examining the data, monthly sales patterns were studied. The analysis shows higher sales in early months and a gradual decline later, helping support better planning and data-driven decisions.
The analysis reveals that sales peaked during the early months of the year (February–March), indicating higher customer demand during this period. From mid-year onwards, a gradual decline in revenue is observed, with the lowest sales recorded during the last quarter of the year. These insights help highlight seasonal sales behavior and can support better business decisions, inventory planning, and marketing strategies.
Key steps in the analysis included: Data cleaning and preprocessing,Aggregating monthly revenue,Identifying sales trends and seasonal patterns,Interpreting business insights from the results
